How to Use A/B Testing

Learn how to create, run, and analyze effective A/B tests for your marketing campaigns

1. Setting Up Tests

When creating a campaign message or promo code, fill in these fields:

  • Variant Name: "A", "B", "20%", "50%", etc. - identifies which version of the test
  • Test Tags: Comma-separated attributes like "emoji,urgency,discount50"
  • Landing URL: Where you want people to go (e.g., https://yoursite.com/sale)
  • UTM Source: Platform name (instagram, tiktok, facebook, email)
  • UTM Medium: Type (social, post, story, ad, email)
  • UTM Campaign: Campaign name (spring_sale_2025)

3. Getting Your Tracking URL

For each campaign message or discount, use the tracking URL in your posts:

https://yoursite.com/sale?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=spring_sale_2025&variant=A&code=SPRING20

The system automatically generates this URL with all your parameters. Use it in your Instagram bio link or stories!

4. How Performance Tracking Works

The system automatically tracks performance through your tracking URLs:

  1. When someone clicks your tracking URL: The UTM parameters tell us which post/variant they came from
  2. When they visit your site: We count that as a "click" for that variant
  3. When they make a purchase: We attribute the conversion and revenue to that variant
  4. The dashboard updates: You see real-time CTR, conversion rates, and revenue for each variant

üí° Make sure to use the tracking URL from the dashboard in your social posts, emails, and ads!

5. Understanding Tags vs Performance

Tags are for organization, NOT for calculation

What tags do: Help you filter and group tests (e.g., find all "emoji" or "urgency" tests)
What tags DON'T do: They don't affect performance calculations at all

Performance is calculated from:

  • Clicks: How many people clicked your tracking URL
  • Conversions: How many made a purchase
  • Revenue: Total sales generated

üí° Compare variants by looking at "A" vs "B" performance, not by tags!

6. Analyzing Results

This dashboard shows you which variants perform best. Look for:

  • High CTR (Click-Through Rate): More people clicking your links
  • High Conversion Rate: More clicks turning into purchases
  • Best Tags: Which content attributes (emoji, urgency, discount amount) work best
  • Winner Badge: Shows the highest-performing variant
