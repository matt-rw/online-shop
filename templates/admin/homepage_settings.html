Image size recommendations & tips

Recommended dimensions:

  • Single image: 1920×1080px or larger (16:9 aspect ratio)
  • Split view - Left (70%): ~1350×1080px
  • Split view - Right (30%): ~580×1080px (shown on mobile)

Recommended file sizes:

  • Target: 150–400 KB per image (ideal for fast loading)
  • Maximum: 1 MB (larger files slow down page load)
  • Format: WebP (smallest), JPG (photos), PNG (transparency only)

Tip: Use squoosh.app or tinypng.com to compress images before uploading.
